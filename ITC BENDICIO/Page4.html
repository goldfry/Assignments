Dumaguete Oslob Whaleshark Tour

Travel from Dumaguete to Cebu and meet up with a friendly driver who will take you to Oslob, home of what locals call the "butanding".
This slow-moving, filter-feeding carpet shark is one of the largest known fish species - and a true sight to behold. You will not only
get to snorkel and swim with the whale sharks, but also participate in the feeding. Since whale shark encounters are only allowed during
the morning (6:30am to 12:30pm), it's best to head out to Cebu early. Arrive at 7:30am at the Liloan Port where your driver will pick you
up and take you for a scenic drive along the Southern Cebu coastline. Once you arrive in Oslob, get your snorkeling gear (provided if you
chose the Swimming Fee Snorkeling with Whale Sharks Add-On) and dive in the water for your whale shark encounter.
